ACADEMICS SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL STRANDS AVAILABLE

The academic track in senior high school focuses on preparing students for higher education. It offers specialized subjects in areas like Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), and Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM). Students choose a strand based on their interests and career goals. This track develops critical thinking, problem-solving, and research skills, providing a strong foundation for college or university studies. It aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for academic success and future professional careers.

Humanities and Social Sciences

TThe HUMSS strand stands for Humanities and Social Sciences. It is an academic track offered to senior high school students, particularly in the Philippines. This strand is designed for those who have an interest in the humanities, social sciences, arts, literature, and culture. It aims to provide students with a well-rounded education that emphasizes critical thinking, communication, cultural understanding, and social awareness. Students in the HUMSS strand engage in a variety of subjects such as literature, history, economics, psychology, sociology, and political science. The curriculum is intended to cultivate well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the necessary skills for further studies or careers in fields related to humanities and social sciences. Choosing the HUMSS strand can lead to potential career pathways in journalism, law, teaching, politics, writing, psychology, and other professions that value the skills and knowledge acquired through this educational track. It's a great choice for students who wish to explore these areas and develop a strong foundation for their future academic and career pursuits.

Accounting, business & management

The ABM strand stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management. It's a track designed for senior high school students as part of the K-12 education system. This strand prepares students for future careers in business and finance by teaching them the basic concepts of financial management, accounting, and corporate operations. Here's a brief overview of what the ABM strand entails: Acquire Basic Industry Skills: It equips students with the necessary skills for higher education or the job market. Adopt the Right Mindset: Students develop critical thinking and teamwork abilities. Gain Business Exposure: Through business simulations and projects, students get a practical understanding of the business world. The ABM strand is ideal for those who aspire to become business leaders, entrepreneurs, or pursue business-related college degrees. It's a comprehensive program that not only focuses on academic knowledge but also on developing the practical skills and mindset needed for success in the business industry.

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

The STEM strand in Senior High School, particularly in the Philippines, stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It is an inquiry and research-based program designed to expose students to more complex concepts in these areas. The curriculum serves as a solid foundation for students planning to pursue college degrees related to STEM fields. Here's a brief overview of what the STEM strand includes: Problem-solving skills: Students engage in hands-on activities and apply the scientific method to hypothesize, experiment, analyze, and draw conclusions. Communication & collaboration: Through group work and presentations, students enhance their communication skills and learn to work effectively with others. Adaptability: The curriculum encourages students to adapt to change and develop resilience through trial-and-error learning experiences. Major Classes: The STEM curriculum is divided into core, contextualized, and specialized subjects, becoming more focused on STEM-related topics in the second year of Senior High School. This strand is particularly beneficial for students who have a keen interest in the sciences and mathematics and are considering a future career in engineering, technology, or research. It aims to develop not only their academic knowledge but also critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaborative skills that are essential in the modern world.

General academic strand

The GAS strand stands for General Academic Strand in senior high school. It's designed for students in the Philippines who are still undecided about their future career path or college degree program. The GAS strand offers a broad curriculum that covers various disciplines, allowing students to explore different fields of study before making a more informed decision about their tertiary education. Here's a brief overview of what the GAS strand includes: Flexibility: Students can choose electives from different strands, which helps them in deciding on a university degree. Wide Range of Subjects: It includes subjects like general mathematics, statistics, probability, philosophy, humanities, organization and management, life science, social science, applied economics, oral communication, and risk reduction and disaster preparedness. Balanced Mix: GAS is a mix of HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences), STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), and ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) strands, providing a balanced education. Preparation for College: The strand prepares students for almost any course in university by providing a solid foundation in a variety of subjects. Students who choose the GAS strand benefit from a rich educational background that can be advantageous when they eventually pick their specialization in college. It's particularly ideal for those leaning towards liberal arts, communication arts, legal management, journalism, or education.
