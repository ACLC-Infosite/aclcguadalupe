AMA Computer Learning Center (ACLC) was founded in 1986 and is a top computer training school in the country. It offers both full 2-year programs and shorter courses. ACLC's goal is to produce highly skilled graduates who can meet the growing needs of local and global job markets. The school provides the most up-to-date curriculum to equip students with the necessary skills for IT careers. ACLC also has international partnerships that allow its graduates to get well-paying jobs and advance in their careers.
